Frequent Questions

How do I change my meal preference? expand_more
You can update your dietary preferences in the Profile settings. Changes made before 4 PM will apply to the next day's meal schedule.
What is the QR ID for? expand_more
Your unique QR ID is used for touchless meal collection at the canteen kiosks. Simply scan your code at the designated terminal to verify your subscription and receive your meal.
Can I pause my subscription? expand_more
Yes, you can pause your subscription for up to 14 days per semester via the 'Subscription' category in this app.
